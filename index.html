03 · The Solution

One app. Every signal. Zero switching.

💰
Finance
Track spending, savings, and budget vs. actuals. Plaid-connected, always live.
🏃
Fitness
Activity rings, step counts, and move points synced from Apple HealthKit.
🥗
Diet
Log meals, track calories and macros, auto-build grocery lists from your diet plan.
✨
The LifeOS difference
All three modules share one live model. A grocery purchase updates your nutrition log and your budget simultaneously. One input — three systems updated in real time.
